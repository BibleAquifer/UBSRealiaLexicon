## Drugged wine, myrrhed wine (id: REALIA:1.15.5)

1\.15\.5 Drugged wine, myrrhed wine
===================================

Reference:
----------

Greek σμυρνίζω, οἶνος (esmurnismenos oinos)

[MRK 15:23](https://ref.ly/Mark15:23)

Description:
------------

Drugged wine was wine mixed with myrrh. It was used as a potion to reduce pain.

---

Translation:
------------

In general the Greek phrase *esmurnismenos oinos* must be rendered by means of a phrase or clause, for example, “wine that has been mixed with a drug called myrrh” or “wine mixed with a drug to reduce pain.” It is also possible to use an abbreviated phrase such as “myrrhed wine” and then to place the explanation of its function in a footnote.

* **Associated Passages:** Mark 15:23
