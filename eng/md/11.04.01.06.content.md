The *qaneh* in Ezekiel was a measuring rod used to measure length. It had a fixed length of six cubits and a palm, that is, about 3\.5 meters (11\.5 feet). See also [1\.8\.6 Measuring rod, measuring line\<REALIA:1\.8\.6\>](#), and [“Reed”\<FLORA:5\.1\.4\>](#) in *Plants and Trees in the Bible*.
