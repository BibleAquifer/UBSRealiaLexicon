## Cushion, pillow (id: REALIA:5.6)

5\.6 Cushion, pillow
====================

References:
-----------

Greek προσκεφάλαιον (proskefalaion)

[MRK 4:38](https://ref.ly/Mark4:38), [1ES 3:8](https://ref.ly/1Esd3:8)

Description and usage:
----------------------

The pillow was an object, usually soft, on which a person would lay his or her head.

---

Translation:
------------

It is important to avoid a translation in [MRK 4:38](https://ref.ly/Mark4:38) that would suggest that Jesus was so small or curled up as to be able to sleep on a single pillow. GECL (German Common Language Version (Gute Nachricht Bibel)), for example, has Jesus lying “on a seat\-cushion.” It is better to say “with his head on a cushion” (NLT (New Living Translation)) or “with his head on a pillow” (GNT (Good News Translation (1992)), CEV (Contemporary English Version)).

* **Associated Passages:** Mark 4:38; 1 Esdras (Greek) 3:8
