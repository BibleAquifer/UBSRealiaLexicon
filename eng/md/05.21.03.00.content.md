## Peg (on a wall) (id: REALIA:5.21.3)

5\.21\.3 Peg (on a wall)
========================

References:
-----------

Hebrew יָתֵד (yathed)

[ISA 22:23](https://ref.ly/Isa22:23), [ISA 22:25](https://ref.ly/Isa22:25), [EZK 15:3](https://ref.ly/Ezek15:3)

Description:
------------

The peg was a short wooden stick, pointed on one end. It was pounded into a crack between stones that formed the wall of a house. It then served as a place to hang objects.

---

Translation:
------------

In [ISA 22:23](https://ref.ly/Isa22:23); [ISA 22:25](https://ref.ly/Isa22:25) the wall peg is a symbol of stability. See the discussion at [3\.2\.2 Tent peg, stake\<REALIA:3\.2\.2\>](#).

* **Associated Passages:** Isaiah 22:23; Isaiah 22:25; Ezekiel 15:3
