## Yogurt, curds (id: REALIA:9.7)

9\.7 Yogurt, curds
==================

References:
-----------

Hebrew חֶמְאָה (chem’ah)

[GEN 18:8](https://ref.ly/Gen18:8), [DEU 32:14](https://ref.ly/Deut32:14), [JDG 5:25](https://ref.ly/Judg5:25), [2SA 17:29](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:29), [JOB 20:17](https://ref.ly/Job20:17), [PSA 55:22](https://ref.ly/Ps55:22), [PRO 30:33](https://ref.ly/Prov30:33), [ISA 7:15](https://ref.ly/Isa7:15), [ISA 7:22](https://ref.ly/Isa7:22), [ISA 7:22](https://ref.ly/Isa7:22)

Hebrew חֵמָה (chemah)

[JOB 29:6](https://ref.ly/Job29:6)

Description:
------------

Yogurt is a semisolid food created when milk has been curdled by bacteria.

---

Translation:
------------

Translations of the Hebrew word *chem’ah* differ considerably. GNT (Good News Translation (1992)) ignores the souring process and translates it as “milk” in [ISA 7:15](https://ref.ly/Isa7:15), while NCV (New Century Version) has “milk curds,” which is probably not well understood by its target audience. CEV (Contemporary English Version) “yogurt” is better.
