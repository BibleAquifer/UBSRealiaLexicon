## Apron (id: REALIA:6.10)

6\.10 Apron
===========

Reference:
----------

Greek σιμικίνθιον (simikinthion)

[ACT 19:12](https://ref.ly/Acts19:12)

Description and usage:
----------------------

![Leather apron](../json/images/WEB-0505_apron_leather.jpg)

*Leather apron (© MikiNikoloski, CC BY\-SA 3\.0, via Wikimedia Commons)*

The apron was a cloth or piece of leather wrapped by a workman around his waist or chest to protect his clothes.

---

Translation:
------------

In some languages “apron” may be rendered “workman’s cloth” or “work­man’s cloth to protect clothing.”

For the meaning of “ephod” as a kind of work apron, see [4\.5\.4 Ephod\<REALIA:4\.5\.4\>](#).

* **Associated Passages:** Acts 19:12
