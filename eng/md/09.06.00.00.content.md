## Soup, stew (id: REALIA:9.6)

9\.6 Soup, stew
===============

References:
-----------

Hebrew מָרַק (maraq)

[JDG 6:19](https://ref.ly/Judg6:19), [JDG 6:20](https://ref.ly/Judg6:20), [ISA 65:4](https://ref.ly/Isa65:4)

Hebrew מֶרְקָחָה (merqachah)

[EZK 24:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek24:10)

Hebrew נָזִיד (nazid)

[GEN 25:29](https://ref.ly/Gen25:29), [GEN 25:34](https://ref.ly/Gen25:34), [2KI 4:38](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:38), [2KI 4:39](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:39), [2KI 4:40](https://ref.ly/2Kgs4:40), [HAG 2:12](https://ref.ly/Hag2:12)

Greek ἕψεμα (hepsēma)

[BEL 1:33](https://ref.ly/Bel1:33)

Description:
------------

Soup was a meal made by boiling vegetables and/or meat in water.

---

Translation:
------------

The Hebrew word *maraq* refers to a stew made with meat, while the word *nazid* indicates a stew made with vegetables or legumes. Some languages will have a distinct word for each one.

* **Associated Passages:** Judges 6:19; Judges 6:20; Isaiah 65:4; Ezekiel 24:10; Genesis 25:29; Genesis 25:34; 2 Kings 4:38; 2 Kings 4:39; 2 Kings 4:40; Haggai 2:12; Bel and the Dragon 1:33
