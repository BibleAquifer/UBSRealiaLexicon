Description:
------------

If Osborn is correct in his conclusion that there were two different tents (see [3\.15 Tent of Meeting and Tabernacle\<REALIA:3\.15\>](#)), then the dimensions given in the Pentateuch refer to the Tabernacle, and we have no actual description given for the Tent of Meeting. It was evidently a small structure, one that a person could set up alone.
