## Eye salve (id: REALIA:1.15.3)

1\.15\.3 Eye salve
==================

Reference:
----------

Greek κολλούριον (kollourion)

[REV 3:18](https://ref.ly/Rev3:18)

Description:
------------

Eye salve was an ointment. It is possible that it was also in the form of a powder.

---

Usage:
------

The salve was applied to the eyes as a medicine.

---

Translation:
------------

Where the equivalent for a specific “eye salve/ointment/ paste” is not available, the Greek word *kollourion* may be rendered simply as “medicine for the eyes.”

* **Associated Passages:** Revelation 3:18

* **Associated ACAI Concepts:** Eye Salve (ID: `realia:EyeSalve`); Clothing (ID: `realia:Clothing`)
