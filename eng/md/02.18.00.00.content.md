## Barrier, barricade, roadblock (id: REALIA:2.18)

2\.18 Barrier, barricade, roadblock
===================================

Reference:
----------

Greek σκάνδαλον (skandalon)

[JDT 5:1](https://ref.ly/Jdt5:1)

Description and usage:
----------------------

The barrier was any structure erected to prevent passage.

---

Translation:
------------

[JDT 5:1](https://ref.ly/Jdt5:1): The Greek word *skandalon* refers literally to the trigger peg in an animal trap. It is used frequently in the New Testament with the meaning “something that causes a person to stumble.” It can also refer to an object that hinders someone’s progress. So NJB (New Jerusalem Bible (1985)) renders it “obstructions” in [JDT 5:1](https://ref.ly/Jdt5:1), and ITCL (Italian Common Language Version) uses the verb phrase “blocked the road.” GNT (Good News Translation (1992)) says “roadblocks.”

* **Associated Passages:** Judith 5:1
