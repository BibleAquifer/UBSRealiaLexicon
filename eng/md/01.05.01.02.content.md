## Glue (id: REALIA:1.5.1.2)

1\.5\.1\.2 Glue
===============

Reference:
----------

Greek συγκολλάω (sugkollaō (verb))

[SIR 22:9](https://ref.ly/Sir22:9)

Description and usage:
----------------------

Glue is an adhesive substance used to stick one thing to another.

---

Translation:
------------

While nearly all languages have a word for adhesive substances, the Greek text of [SIR 22:7](https://ref.ly/Sir22:7) actually uses a verb, and many translations will prefer to describe the action of sticking two things back together; for example, GNT (Good News Translation (1992)) has “Trying to teach a fool is like gluing a broken pot back together ….” Some languages may distinguish between different kinds of adhesives. The glue chosen here should be appropriate for sticking together pieces of earthenware pottery.

* **Associated Passages:** Sirach 22:9; Sirach 22:7
