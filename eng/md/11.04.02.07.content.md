## Measuring rod (id: REALIA:11.4.2.7)

11\.4\.2\.7 Measuring rod
=========================

References:
-----------

Greek κάλαμος (kalamos)

[REV 11:1](https://ref.ly/Rev11:1), [REV 21:15](https://ref.ly/Rev21:15), [REV 21:16](https://ref.ly/Rev21:16)

Like the *qaneh* (see above), the *kalamos* was a measuring rod. In Revelation its length is not given but only the lengths of the things it measures. See also [1\.8\.6 Measuring rod, measuring line\<REALIA:1\.8\.6\>](#).
