## Span (id: REALIA:11.4.1.3)

11\.4\.1\.3 Span
================

References:
-----------

Hebrew זֶרֶת (zereth)

[EXO 28:16](https://ref.ly/Exod28:16), [EXO 28:16](https://ref.ly/Exod28:16), [EXO 39:9](https://ref.ly/Exod39:9), [EXO 39:9](https://ref.ly/Exod39:9), [1SA 17:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:4), [ISA 40:12](https://ref.ly/Isa40:12), [EZK 43:13](https://ref.ly/Ezek43:13)

With the fingers spread, the *zereth* is the distance from the tip of the thumb to the tip of the little finger.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ISA 40:12](https://ref.ly/Isa40:12): The picture here is of a person stretching out his fingers and using the distance between his thumb and little finger to measure the extent of the sky. For the second clause in this verse, GECL (German Common Language Version (Gute Nachricht Bibel)) has “Who can … determine the extent of the sky with the span of his hand.”

* **Associated Passages:** Exodus 28:16; Exodus 39:9; 1 Samuel 17:4; Isaiah 40:12; Ezekiel 43:13
