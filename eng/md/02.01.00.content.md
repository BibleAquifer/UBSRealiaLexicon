Description:
------------

The Hebrew words in the passages listed above are generic words for “weapon.”

---

Translation:
------------

The Hebrew word *kli* is a general term meaning “implement,” “tool,” or “vessel,” but in the passages listed above it is best rendered “weapon\[s].”

The Hebrew word *shelach* refers to weapons that are thrown or projected, such as javelins and arrows.
