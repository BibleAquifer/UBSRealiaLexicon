## Hall, auditorium (id: REALIA:3.18)

3\.18 Hall, auditorium
======================

Reference:
----------

Greek ἀκροατήριον (akroatērion)

[ACT 25:23](https://ref.ly/Acts25:23)

Description and usage:
----------------------

The auditorium was a relatively large building normally used for legal hearings, though possibly also employed for more general purposes.

---

Translation:
------------

In a number of languages “auditorium” may be rendered “large hall,” “place where many people could gather together,” or “building where many people could sit to listen.”

* **Associated Passages:** Acts 25:23

* **Associated ACAI Concepts:** Audience Hall (ID: `realia:AudienceHall`)
