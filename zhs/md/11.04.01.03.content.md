[EXO 28:16](https://ref.ly/Exod28:16), [EXO 28:16](https://ref.ly/Exod28:16), [EXO 39:9](https://ref.ly/Exod39:9), [EXO 39:9](https://ref.ly/Exod39:9), [1SA 17:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:4), [ISA 40:12](https://ref.ly/Isa40:12), [EZK 43:13](https://ref.ly/Ezek43:13)

[ISA 40:12](https://ref.ly/Isa40:12) ：这里描绘的图景是，一个人张开手指，用拇指和小指之间的距离来衡量穹苍。对于这节经文的第二个分句，GECL (German Common Language Version (Gute Nachricht Bibel)) 意为“谁能用虎口确定天的大小呢”。
